## HOW TO USE THIS DIRECTORY

1. **Choose your target** based on your specific concern
2. **Use FOIA templates** (see FOIA_TEMPLATES.md)
3. **Send via multiple channels** (email + physical letter)
4. **CC civil society orgs** (creates witnesses)
5. **Follow up in 7 days** (FOIA requires response)
6. **Escalate to SERAP** if denied
7. **Share publicly** that you sent (Twitter, Nairaland)

---

**Your rights under FOIA 2011:**
- Agencies must respond in 7 days
- You can sue if denied without valid exemption
- Courts can compel disclosure
- You cannot be arrested for asking questions

**Use these contacts. Demand accountability.**
